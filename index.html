Hola!

I am a documentary filmmaker and web developer. I am the creative director and cofounder of Advanced Apes. In my spare time, I hug dragons as a side hustle. There's a huge demand for dragon hugging btw!

My Skills.

Video Editing

I started learning how to video edit when I was 10 years old. After seeing Casey Neistat's vlogs, I documented my 13 year old life everyday for a year. It taught me how to edit, tell great stories, and stay committed.

Cinematography

Cinematography is the art of camera movement. Dynamic camera movements drive the story forward. Without great cinematography, the film is left motionless, and ultimately, lifeless. I love finding new and innovative ways to practice camera jitsu. If you need help driving home your story, I'm exactly the man for that.

Photography

I started to take pictures when I was 10 years old. An older friend of mine gave me a camera and it soon became my best friend. I took it everywhere using it wherever I went. Soon, I learned how to create beautiful compositions that tell a story through the lens.

Graphic Design

After photography, I began to learn how to use Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Another beautiful relationship emerged and graphic design became my next love. I would load the photos onto my laptop and spend hours editing them. Ultimately, it is the process, not the results, that are most important. It is in the process that we uncover a treasure trove of firey passion to fuel our evolution of art.

Web Development

My friend, Trevor, told me that web development was an important skill to learn in today's 21st century world. I agreed and decided to learn how to code. I fell in love with the process and it became another one of my skills. Creation is the name of the game.

Languages

I knew two languages at a young age: English and Vietnamese. Vietnamese was actually my first language and I spoke it boldly.... Until the day I stopped speaking it. The language became weaker and less used. Then, in 2015, by myself, I decided to study Vietnamese again. Language acqusition soon became another passion of mine. Currently, I'm in the process of acquiring Spanish!

Writing

Writing is another passion that fuels my life. I love piecing words together to create the best delicious meal. The love of telling stories, to drive a message home, makes me fulfilled in every way possible. I love the process of creation.

Master Chef

In the end, my best skill is being a Master Chef. I am better than Gordon Ramsey and can cook 5 savory meals in 5 minutes. LOL, not really, but I can cook a banging meal. I love cooking for close friends and family members. There's nothing quite like the experience of eating with those whom you love. Sharing a meal and basking in the moment is truly a gift. In life, it's the small moments that matter. Because the small moments make up what is called Life.
